about me


Hi, I'm Anna! I'm currently a student at the University of Pennsylvania studying computer graphics through the Digital Media Design program.

Outside of class, I enjoy learning new things whenever I can, such as cooking and embroidery, and I also enjoy running and swimming recreationally!
