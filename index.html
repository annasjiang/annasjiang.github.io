about me

Hi, I'm Anna! I'm currently a student at the University of Pennsylvania studying Digital Media Design.

I've been an artist my whole life, but I just started learning computer science this past year! I'm really excited to learn more about CS, and I've coded this website so I could familiarize myself with more languages over summer break! I'm interested in combining my design and computer science talents and creating projects that have an impact on others — whether it be big or small.

Outside of class, I enjoy learning new things whenever I can, such as cooking and embroidery, and I also enjoy running and swimming recreationally!
