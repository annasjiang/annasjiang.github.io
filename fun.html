Just For Fun!

I absolutely love all forms of art and I often dedicate my breaks to learning new mediums or taking on long term passion projects. Here are a few of my recent ventures!

Pantone Postcards

    I've always had a tradition with my hometown friends to send each other postcards whenever we go on vacation. When the pandemic hit, I still wanted to keep the tradition alive in somehow. So, I bought a pack of Pantone postcards and decided to paint them for a few of my friends. I posted them on Instagram and so many people loved them that I started taking commissions for custom postcards. I'm also selling a few of them as prints and stickers here!
