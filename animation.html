ANIMATION

When I first started running in quarantine, it was hard to keep myself from thinking about how every muscle was basically on fire. So, I focused on the music blasting in my headphones. I began crafting lyric videos and envisioning animated elements flying through the sky as I ran until one day, I decided to bring my imagination to life.
